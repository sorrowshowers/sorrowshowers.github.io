Plushies & the Mundane

Published on April 24th, 2025 by Ajax Freyr

Dear Internet,
I want to share the whimsy I’ve been cultivating in my life as of recently.

For a couple of months, I’ve been trying to make my dorm actually mine. Personalizing my own space is something I’ve been struggling with for a couple years, since I’ve been moving all over the state (and country!) ever since 2021. I hang up hella stuff on the walls, have books on my shelves, display my accessories on the vanity, etcetera… but my bed is ALWAYS barren. So when the school year started, I brought up some of my plushies from home. Let me introduce you to my new fuzzy friends, and to the gijinkas I made of them!

Click on the arrows to expand their description.

Knuffle "Knuff" Bunny

Knuff holds a very special place in my heart. My mom bought me a bunny plush when I was an infant, and read the original Knuffle Bunny book to me countless times throughout my childhood. He’s been with me for over 20 years now! I honestly can’t believe he doesn’t look like absolute crap. I included the yellow and blue from the original book color scheme. I also gave him a blonde bob because that’s how I used to look as a kid, lol.


Squeak the Pachamari & Squeeze the Tangela

I keep these two together on the bed, shelf, or desk wherever I move. I bought Squeak for myself, and Squeeze was a Christmas gift from my mom. They’ve been a staple in my rooms throughout the years… I love them so much. As you can probably guess, Squeak squeaks when squeezed. Which is why I named them Squeak and Squeeze. Ugh, too many words. Anyway, I think they’d be childhood buds that play in the dirt and eat mud cakes together. Maybe I’ll draw them more soon!


Capsaicin the Pikmin

I originally got them to impress my ex from when I was like, 14, who was super into Pikmin. LOL. I still love my lil guy, though. I keep them around to store my extra piercing jewelry in. They’re a real rockstar to me. I like how I gave them a mullet kinda getup where their stem meets their head. And the striped bangs sell the look for sure.


Bubbles the Clefairy

I snagged her from Walmart when I was moving between my summer and fall housing in 2024. I had shipped a bunch of my stuff back home because I was moving from an apartment to a single dorm, so I was sad that I didn’t have many plushies. Thus, I took the softest one home. She’s adorable, and my favorite to hug while I drift off to sleep. She’s real solid too, so she serves as a good pillow when I need it. I gave her a prehensile tail and spiky hair to complement her round design (especially that front bit of hair that’s spiral-y). Overall I feel like she’d be a simple gal, so I gave her a simple design.


Crash the Shark

My most recent purchase, actually! I love him so much, he’s really nice to scream into and hug.I made him a super cool surfer dude who has a pet shark. But Crash is actually the shark, he’s just using the surfer dude as a vessel. Venom/Eddie Brock type situation. I also gave him a lil tutu because he’s comfortable in his masculinity :)


I think of all my plushies as a big family. Crash and Bubbles as the parents, Capsaicin as the edgy teen, and the rest being kids. Oh god, I can feel the lore forming in my head…

Other than giving unnecessary lore to my plushies, I’ve been trying to appreciate the small details in life more. You know, the mundane and ordinary stuff that I otherwise wouldn’t notice if I hadn’t had more time on my hands.

    Things I've noticed recently:
  • The patterns shifting on my roommate’s bed when the afternoon sun shines through our blinds
  • How my crocs squeak when I walk
  • My friends’ subtle vocal changes when they start talking about something or someone they love
  • The rotation of trinkets my roommate leaves on her vanity and desk
  • How the tall grasses near the pond grow greener and greener every day

There are always more observations to be had in the mundane. And I can’t wait to discover them as I move through life. Maybe I’ll include a list of my ordinary observations at the end of each weblog… if I can remember it.

That’s all for today. I hope everyone is enjoying the nice weather!
